# Video Banner Assets

Place your video file here for the news banner.

## Requirements:
- **Format**: MP4 (recommended) or WebM
- **Duration**: ~5 seconds (will loop automatically)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 recommended
- **File Name**: `news-banner.mp4`

## Video Content:
The video should showcase news events happening across India, such as:
- Breaking news scenes
- Political rallies
- Cultural events
- Economic activities
- Social gatherings
- Newsroom activities

## File Size:
Keep the video file size optimized (under 5MB recommended) for faster loading.

## Usage:
Once you place your video file here as `news-banner.mp4`, it will automatically be used in the video banner component.
